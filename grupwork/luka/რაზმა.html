cats


Cats are domestic animals. They come in many breeds.

There are several breeds of cats and they are all very interesting and people love them very much

For example, the Scottish Fold The Scottish Fold is a breed of cat with a dominant mutation that causes the ear cartilage to have a fold, resulting in the ears being pointed forward and drooping. This is why the breed is often said to have an owl-like appearance.[1] Originally called the lop-eared cat, the Scottish Fold was named in 1966. Depending on the breed's registration, long-haired Folds may be referred to as the Highland Fold, Long-haired Scottish Fold, Long-haired Fold, and Couper.[2]
